The Sauce

What is Chicaoji?

A medium-heat hot sauce woven from three superfoods. Equally at home on eggs, on salmon, in stew, and folded into brownies. Six tastes, one bottle.

Chicaoji is a medium-heat hot sauce based on three superfoods — goji berries, raw cacao, and chipotles — held together with apple cider vinegar, water, organic maple syrup, and a pinch of Celtic Sea Salt®. That's the whole list.

The flavor is unlike any other hot sauce. Chipotles give it warmth and smoke. Cacao adds a roasted, slightly bitter depth. Goji brings a natural sweetness that lets the sauce sit comfortably on dessert as easily as dinner. The result is a condiment that quietly does six things at once — sweet, sour, salt, bitter, savory, spicy — without any of them dominating.

The Ingredient List

In descending order by weight:

  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Water
  • Goji berries
  • Chipotles (smoked jalapeño chilies)
  • Organic maple syrup, sourced from Maple Valley Cooperative
  • Celtic Sea Salt®
  • Cacao nibs

No preservatives. No flavorings. No colorings. No additives of any kind.
