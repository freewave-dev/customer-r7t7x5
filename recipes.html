Quick Ideas

If you're staring at the fridge.

Start with one of these. Most take less than five seconds.

  • A drop on scrambled eggs.
  • Drizzled over avocado toast.
  • Stirred into a bowl of rice and beans.
  • Brushed onto roasted sweet potatoes.
  • Whisked with olive oil for a quick dressing.
  • Spooned over vanilla ice cream. Trust us.
